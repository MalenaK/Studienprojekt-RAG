Test Case Number: 4
Question: How can a defensive player establish a legal guarding position?
Actual Answer: According to the FIBA Official Basketball Rules 2020, a defensive player can establish an initial legal guarding position in the following way:

* He must face his opponent (Section 33.3, ..\data\data_basic\FIBAOfficialBasketballRules2020_v1.0.pdf:38:1)
* He must have both feet on the floor (Section 33.3, ..\data\data_basic\FIBAOfficialBasketballRules2020_v1.0.pdf:38:1)

Additionally, the defensive player must maintain his arms and hands within a vertical position inside an imaginary cylinder that extends from the floor to the ceiling above him, even when raising them above his head or jumping vertically (Section 33.3, ..\data\data_basic\FIBAOfficialBasketballRules2020_v1.0.pdf:38:1).

It is also important to note that when guarding a player who controls the ball, the defensive player must establish an initial legal guarding position without causing contact before taking his position (Section 33.4, ..\data\data_basic\FIBAOfficialBasketballRules2020_v1.0.pdf:39:0).

Actual Answer: To stop the engine, follow these steps:

1. Release the throttle and pull back on both steering levers at the same time (from TM 9-759, page 10).

This shuts off the fuel from the idle fuel supply wells in the carburetor and prevents loading up of the carburetor in case the engine is to be restarted while still hot (from ..\data\data_basic\m4a3.pdf, page 18).

Please note that these instructions are for a M4A3 tank.
