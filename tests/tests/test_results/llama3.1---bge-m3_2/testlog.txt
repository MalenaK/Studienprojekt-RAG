Test Case Number: 5
Question: May a team substitute a player?
Actual Answer: According to the FIBA Official Basketball Rules 2020 (Article 19, Substitution), yes, a team may substitute a player(s) during a substitution opportunity.

A substitution opportunity begins when:

* The ball becomes dead and the game clock is stopped.
* The ball becomes dead following a successful last free throw.
* For the non-scoring team, a field goal is scored when the game clock shows 2:00 minutes or less in the fourth quarter and in each overtime. (Source of info in this block: ..\data\data_basic\FIBAOfficialBasketballRules2020_v1.0.pdf:26:0)

The team may substitute players during these opportunities, and a player who has become a substitute and a substitute who has become a player cannot re-enter the game until the ball becomes dead again after a clock-running phase of the game, unless the team is reduced to fewer than 5 players on the playing court.
